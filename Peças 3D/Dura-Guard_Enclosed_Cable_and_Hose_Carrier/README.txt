# Summary

Thread cable and hose through these carriers—they support and protect moving cable and hose. The open design promotes air flow to prevent heat buildup and keeps cable and hose visible. Connect lengths of carrier without tools—snap the links together to create the exact length you need. Disassemble them with a screwdriver.
